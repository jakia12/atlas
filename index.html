How To Buy

Make Wallet

Download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Get Some ETH

Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $ATLAS. If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go To Uniswap

Visit app.uniswap.org in Google Chrome or the Metamask browser. Connect your wallet, paste the $ATLAS token address, select Atlas, and confirm. Sign the wallet signature when prompted by Metamask.

Swap ETH for $ATLAS

Swap ETH for $ATLAS. There are ZERO taxes, so you don't need to worry about a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during market volatility.
